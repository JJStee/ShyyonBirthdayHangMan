Instructions

This is a simple Hangman game. The goal is to guess the word sequence by suggesting individual letters one at a time.

  • Each time you guess a correct letter, it will be revealed in the word.
  • If you guess an incorrect letter, a part of the hangman will be drawn.
  • You can only make seven incorrect guesses before the hangman is completed. The sequence is then displayed.

These word sequences provide accurate and factual descriptions of yourself.
